About us


Greetings! We, the Grade 10 students of APEC SCHOOLS Ortigas Extension Branch, most of the members above are the founders of LIA (Liberty Information Administration), but now we are called Septem Finanza. We are excited to share with you our latest project in TLE-Life Labs for this school year. This subject aims to equip us with the skills and values needed to develop social innovation by integrating people, ideas, and resources that promote universal values. As part of our responsibility, we are dedicated to safeguarding and improving the financial well-being of organizations and individuals under our jurisdiction. This important role requires us to carry out various tasks with precision and careful consideration.




Our Mission
Our mission is to provide individuals and organizations with the necessary financial knowledge to make informed decisions and achieve their financial goals with confidence. We strive to empower our clients by simplifying complex financial concepts and providing practical solutions tailored to their unique needs.
Our Vision
Our vision is to be able to produce useful and high-quality works in the realm of financial education and literacy. Through our efforts, we aim to equip individuals with the necessary knowledge and skills to effectively manage their finances, ultimately enabling them to build a more prosperous and secure financial future for both themselves and the communities they belong to.
Our Values

1. Integrity: We are committed to providing accurate and unbiased financial information and advice.

2. Inclusivity: We believe that everyone deserves access to financial knowledge, regardless of their background or financial status.

3. Innovation: We are constantly innovating to find new and better ways to deliver financial education.

4. Collaboration: We believe that the best way to learn about finance is through collaboration with others.


To begin, we have chosen to explore the fascinating realm of E-money or Electronic money currency. This field offers exciting opportunities to explore the limitless possibilities of a digitalized financial system. Through this exploration, we hope to gain valuable insights and knowledge that will enable us to contribute to a better and more sustainable future.
